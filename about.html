About Falling Feathers Hollow

← Back to Home

Falling Feathers Hollow is a place of second chances — for animals and people alike. Nestled in the quiet hills of West Virginia, we rescue ducks, chickens, and other farm animals in need of a new beginning.

Our sanctuary also provides space for veterans and families to reconnect with nature, heal through community, and build a simpler, purpose-driven life. From tiny homes to handmade crafts, every part of this project is built with care.

We believe that even the smallest creatures deserve a safe place to land. And that sometimes, healing starts with a feather.
